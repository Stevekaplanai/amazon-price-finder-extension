DETAILED DESCRIPTION:

Stop overpaying! Amazon Price Finder automatically detects products on any website and instantly shows you Amazon prices.

KEY FEATURES:

Auto-Detect Products
Browse Walmart, Best Buy, Target, or any store - we'll automatically detect the product and find it on Amazon. No copying and pasting needed!

Price Alerts
Set your target price and get notified when it drops. Never miss a deal again! Alerts work in the background, even when Chrome is closed.

Price History
Track how prices change over time. See the lowest, average, and highest prices with a visual chart. Make informed buying decisions.

Multi-Marketplace Support
Search across 6 Amazon marketplaces:
- Amazon.com (US)
- Amazon.co.uk (UK)
- Amazon.de (Germany)
- Amazon.fr (France)
- Amazon.ca (Canada)
- Amazon.co.jp (Japan)

Side Panel Interface
Clean, modern side panel that doesn't interrupt your browsing. View results, manage alerts, and check history all in one place.

4. Set alerts for price drops
5. Save money!
